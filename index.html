Instructions

Always use the cold wallet generator offline. Download the latest release on a usb drive and cut your internet connection.

After producing enough number of cold wallets, print them out and clear your printer memory.

Cold wallets are the same with your regular wallet so treat them as such. If you like the generator, please support the developer.
